About ME

Full Stack Developer

My name is Juan Ribeiro Rodrigues, and I'm a 23-year-old student at IFSP Campus SP. I'm currently studying technology in systems analysis and development, with the goal of becoming a developer.

Although I don't have extensive professional experience in the field yet, I did work as a young apprentice in the billing department for eight months, which gave me valuable insights into how businesses operate.

In addition to my studies, I'm also learning English and Japanese, with a beginner level in both languages. I believe that knowing multiple languages will help me communicate with a broader range of clients and collaborators in the future.

As a budding developer, I'm eager to learn and improve my skills, and I'm excited about the possibilities of creating innovative solutions that can make a positive impact on people's lives. I'm always looking for opportunities to apply my knowledge and collaborate with others in the tech industry, so feel free to get in touch if you have any projects or ideas that I could be a part of.
